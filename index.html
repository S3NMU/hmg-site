About HMG

Himilo Management Group (HMG) is a private company registered with the Ministry of Commerce and Industry of the Federal Government of Somalia and has regional offices in the Federal Member States. HMG provides business solutions through innovation and entrepreneurship ecosystem, research and technology, skills training and workflow management. HMG offers business facilitation and linkages to create one-stop-shop services for entrepreneurial support, trade facilitation, investment climate, mentoring and advisory services. HMG helps existing and start-up companies as well as individual entrepreneurs to develop their business through consultancy, capacity development & training, and business incubator.
